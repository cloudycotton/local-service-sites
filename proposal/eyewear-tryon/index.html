Acceptance gate

Targets become tests.

The requested latency and pupil-offset thresholds are treated as measurable acceptance criteria. Before implementation, we agree on the representative devices, sample images, browser versions, and frame artwork. Results are reported, not invented.

A Camera and upload flows work on agreed current mobile and desktop browsers.
B Latency is logged on the agreed mid-range mobile device and optimized against the target.
C Pupil offset is calculated against labelled fixtures and supplied with the test results.
